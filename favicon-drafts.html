Favicon Drafts

Ten directions using the current dark green background and golden key. The first lane is cleaner and app-icon-like; the second leans into an eight-point Islamic star / rosette motif.

Direction 7 Refinement

7A. Clean Mihrab Key

Round 1 reduces the original line clutter and makes the key silhouette stronger.

7B. Polished Mihrab Seal

Round 2 tightens the arch, centers the star, and keeps the key readable at 32px.

7C. Pure Mihrab Key

Removes the star entirely so the key can sit cleanly inside the arch.

Minimal Hybrids

1. Star Frame Key

Closest to the current icon, with a subtle khatim star framing the key.

2. Star Core Key

The key sits inside a tighter star medallion for stronger small-size recognition.

3. Tilted Unlock

A more dynamic key with a faint star behind it; still simple at favicon size.

4. Vertical Seal Key

Symmetrical, calmer, and very legible as a small app icon.

5. Green Star Trace

Adds a little brand green to the star while keeping the key fully gold.

Direction 10 Alternatives

10A. Open Mihrab Key

The original #10 idea with the star removed and the key left diagonal.

10B. Layered Mihrab Key

A more architectural arch, with the key bolder and centered in the niche.

10C. Portal Key

A heavier portal outline with a lower diagonal key and simple baseline detail.

Ornamental Hybrids

6. Full Rosette Key

The most explicit eight-point-star direction; ornate but still icon-shaped.

7. Mihrab Star Key

Cleaned-up direction: mihrab frame, centered star, and a clearer golden key.

8. Rosette Seal Key

Premium and centered; likely the strongest ornamental favicon candidate.

9. Mosaic Compass

Uses tile-grid construction lines behind the key for an architectural feel.

10. Mihrab Tile

The architectural option with the star removed so the diagonal key can breathe.
