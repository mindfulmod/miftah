Bee — a true metamorphosis

Three distinct life forms, not three sizes of one bee: Larva → Pupa → Bee. Clay v2 with layered shading, ambient occlusion, speculars and contact shadows on a real oasis.

Stage words: نَحْل (bee) is earned across the arc; here Larva = نَحْل, Pupa = عَسَل (honey), Bee = شِفَاء (healing).
Each stage is unlocked by a memory milestone — the cocoon literally "opens" into the winged bee as the word reaches mastery.
